About

I am a cybersecurity professional seeking careers in cyber operations and ethical hacking. I am a recipient of the Department of Defense Cyber Scholarship, have participated in the National Cyber League, and have spent time increasing my own learning in skills on websites such as TryHackMe and HackTheBox. I have my CompTIA Security+ certification and am working towards my CISSP. My senior design project had me and my team develop a remotely accessible leave-behind device that can be used to gain attack vectors and exploitations on networks.

In my free time, I teach myself game design, and make my own video games. I have a number of in-development solo projects, but I have also submitted games for game jams visible on itch.io. I and my team's game titled "The Blue Screen" earned 10th place at the Brackey's 2023 Game Jam. During my time working at the Marine Corps Warfighting Lab, I worked closely with the wargaming department to create realistic models and simulations used for data acquisition.
